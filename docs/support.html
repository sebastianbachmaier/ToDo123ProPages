Frequently Asked Questions

‚úÖ How do I create a new todo?

Simply type your todo in the text field at the bottom of the screen and tap the "+" button. You can also add photos, tag contacts using the @ symbol, and set due dates before creating your todo.

‚úèÔ∏è How do I edit or delete a todo?

To edit a todo, tap on it and select the edit option. To delete, swipe left on the todo item and tap the delete button. You can also mark todos as complete by tapping the checkbox next to them.

ÔøΩ How do folders work?

Folders help you organize your todos into categories like Work, Personal, Shopping, etc. Tap the folder icon at the top to create a new folder, and swipe left on a todo to move it to a specific folder. You can also filter todos by folder using the dropdown at the top.

ÔøΩüì∏ How do I add photos to a todo?

When creating or editing a todo, tap the camera icon to take a new photo, or tap the image icon to select photos from your library. You can attach multiple photos to a single todo. Tap on any attached photo to view it in full screen.

üë• How do I tag contacts in my todos?

While typing a todo, use the @ symbol followed by a contact name (e.g., "@John"). The app will show contact suggestions that you can tap to tag. Tagged contacts appear as chips on your todo, and you can tap them to view the contact details or call them directly.

üìÖ How do I set due dates?

Tap the calendar icon when creating a todo to set a due date. You can choose quick options like "Today", "Tomorrow", or select a custom date. Todos with due dates will show when they're due, and overdue todos will be highlighted.

üîÑ How do I reorder my todos?

Long press on any todo item and drag it to your desired position. This allows you to prioritize your tasks in the order that makes sense for you. The order is saved automatically.

üîç How do I filter and find todos?

Use the filter bar at the top to filter todos by contact, due date, or completion status. You can also switch between folders to view todos in specific categories. Multiple filters can be applied simultaneously to narrow down your search.

üì∏ Why does ToDo123Pro need access to my camera and photos?

ToDo123Pro allows you to attach photos to your todos for better context and organization. You can take a photo directly or select existing images from your photo library to associate with your tasks. This permission is only used when you choose to add photos to your todos.

üë• Why does the app need access to my contacts?

The contacts permission allows you to link contacts to specific todos, making it easy to keep track of tasks related to people you know. For example, you can associate a todo like "Call John about the meeting" directly with John's contact information. This feature is completely optional and only activates when you choose to add contacts to a todo.

üîí Where is my data stored?

All your todos, photos, and associated data are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload, sync, or transmit your personal data to any external servers or cloud services. Your privacy is our priority - what stays on your device, stays on your device.

‚òÅÔ∏è Can I back up my data?

Since all data is stored locally, your todos will be included in your device's regular backup (iCloud or iTunes backup). However, there is currently no in-app cloud sync feature, which means your data remains completely private and under your control.

üåê Does the app support multiple languages?

Yes! ToDo123Pro supports multiple languages. You can change the language in the app settings to match your preference.

üé® Can I customize the app's appearance?

Yes! The app supports both light and dark themes. You can access theme settings from the settings tab to choose your preferred appearance.
