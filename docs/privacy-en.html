In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Application.

This Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.


Categories of Personal Data and Purposes

  • Contact data (only with your consent): selected entries from your address book to provide contact suggestions/quick selection within the app.
  • Media content (only selected by you): e.g., photos/images you attach to tasks — strictly to provide the feature within the app; no transfer to our servers.
  • Camera data: live camera access to take photos; captures are processed/stored locally only if you save or attach them.
  • SDK note: Mobile apps may use SDKs instead of cookies to collect diagnostics and performance data.

Permissions and Device Features

Contacts: Access only after your explicit consent in the operating system. Contact information is used solely to provide the corresponding in-app feature. No transfer outside your device takes place.

Photos/Media: Access only with your consent. Images you select are processed and stored only locally on your device; no transfer to our servers.

Camera: Access only with your consent. Captures are stored only locally on your device; no transfer to our servers.

Note: System or cloud backups of your device (e.g., iCloud/Google Backup) are outside our control/responsibility.

  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent): access to Contacts, Photos/Media, and Camera, and any other optional permissions — revocable at any time with effect for the future.
